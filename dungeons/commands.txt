------commands----

w - move up
s - move down
a - move left
d - move right
quit - close the game
e / eat - eat the food
enter/< - climb stair up
enter/> - climb stair down
i - inventory
p / healtpot - healthpot
t / teleport - teleport
? / help - help menu
esc / escape - flee to a random position
c / check / sniff - check monster stats
